Psychic Cloud

Community based, Psychic conferencing startup powered by MSAP

Summary

Mission

Psychic Cloud, as a brand, is a startup created by the parent company, Voice Systems Engineering. Its purpose is to be the skin of a new technological communication platform consisting of digital products and interfaces all working together for maximum user flexibility. This platform is codenamed ‘MSAP’, and is in preparation for the multimillion-dollar 35-year-old psychic reading service called Psychic Source to be transferred over or run alongside Psychic Cloud it if it becomes viable.

MSAP exists as an IOS app, an Android app, a responsive website, and a management admin. It is a combination of conferencing technology (video, chat, audio) with many features, the top being the ability to schedule appointments, live audio ‘events’, AI advisors, AI decision-making tools, and social content creation in the form of video or audio reels. The pay structure is dynamic and dependent upon the feature used.

Depending upon which application/device the user chooses, the experiences are the same. Users can exist/toggle between 3 different roles, a ‘consumer’, an ‘advisor’, or an ‘admin’ inside the chosen app. Advisors always start as consumers who have applied through the vetting process feature built into the app. Admins are only internally created, and they can manage and ‘see’ anything the other roles can via support viewing as functionality.

Psychic Cloud, as a startup brand, continues to seek viability. It has marketing, paid social media advertising, landing pages, special attention given to the onboarding process, and curated psychic content creation.

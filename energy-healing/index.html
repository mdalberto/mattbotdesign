Summary

Mission

The Energy Healing Guide is a compilation of information and resources written by experts in the field. From a business perspective, its purpose is to acquire SEO value and, second, to funnel viewers to the parent site to schedule energy healing style calls with experts.

My Contributions

This project showcases a collaboration with an SEO company to create personas. Experts provided a copy. I created nearly everything else with loose stakeholder interaction. The process proceeded by making wireframes for mobile, exploring patterns, photography, and iconography creation. Making 3 homepage design options as a stand-in for mood boards for stakeholders. After the direction was approved, I designed a template to handle the sections and created the subpage UI and sub-branding with imagery. Upon finishing design, I switched to developer mode to build out (with the ASP.NET) source code in .NET, HTML, and SCSS for the partial page views and views needed for deployment. This was done working with a Scrum team sprint. After launch, I continued involvement with optimization tests to expand the site's business importance. Specifically, multiple A/B tests using Optimizely software. Conclusive results evolved the hero images to have CTA's placed on them and in various other locations, as well as the FAB "Energy Advisor" button.

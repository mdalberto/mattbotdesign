Mission

"OE Web", "Financial Portal", "Expert View", and "Return Call" are a collection of internal web-based tools used by finance, fraud, and customer service to manage customer accounts and interactions. Features for customer support include audio call connection with the customer, new customer account creation led by an onboarding script interface, searching other accounts, viewing dashboards of information, connecting to an advisor via a button press, adding dollars, applying promotions, viewing purchase history, and performing refunds. Fraud and finance features are managing aliases and suspending accounts that perform chargebacks to banks, searching credit card information, and more.

My Contributions

Converting desktop apps to web-based ones with success metrics being reducing fraud and increasing positive call disposition data. Led interview sessions with agents and finance to understand needs. Documented 100+ user story requirements with the project manager. Designed high-fi mockups and prototypes for the development team and created a cohesive design system to speed up productivity and enable team design expansion.

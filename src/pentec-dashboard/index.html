Pentec Health Portal

Specialty Care Electronic Prescribing of Controlled Substances (EPCS)

Summary

Mission

Pentec Health, based near Philadelphia, is a privately owned healthcare company that focuses on providing complex care for specific groups of patients. Their specialty pharmacies prepare custom-made treatments for more than 11,000 active patients in their Specialty Care and Clinical Nutrition & GI Health programs.

My Contributions

As an UI designer, I was tasked with designing two web portals, Specialty Care Customer Portal and Specialty Care EPCS. I met with stakeholders to gather requirements and researched the existing system using the documentation provided. I translated rough outlines and a provided brand guide into high fidelity mockups using Figma. The final deliverables included overall framework "look and feel", key page designs, prototyped flows where needed, and a design pattern of widgets and inputs that development team assembled into the interfaces.
